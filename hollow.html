Hollow

You promised a palace

A fortress
enveloping my dream
of what could be

Three words
thirteen letters

I care about you

Cemented in the jagged stone of my mind
etched with the frailest tools

How ignorant I was
to assume that your words were as thick as
bricks
as strong as
marble pillars
able to withstand the sands of time

How wise I am to have realized
that without the nuts, bolts, and plaster
Of expressive devotion

We erode
